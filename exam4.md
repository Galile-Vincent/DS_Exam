Key Concepts to Remember
========================

* • **AVL Tree**: Self-balancing BST where heights of subtrees differ by at most 1.
* • **Red-Black Tree**: Balanced BST with properties ensuring no path is more than twice as long as any other.
* • **Rotation**: Operation used to rebalance trees (LL, RR, LR, RL).
* • **Balance Factor**: In AVL, height(left) - height(right).
* • **Red-Black Pointers**: Red pointers represent items within the same 2-3-4 node.
